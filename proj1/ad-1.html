The Apocalypse

The Apocalypse was a series of 15 woodcut prints that were bound together. Dürer finished this work around 1498. It was one of his most significant and influential early works, finished just three years after opening his workshop. This book has the distinction of being the first book to be illustrated and published by a major artist.
